A brief introduction to pi:

Pi is a special number that has lot of applications in mathematics. Pi is an irrational number that never ends, and is defined as the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter. The diameter of a circle is twice the radius, so C=2*pi*r, where C is the circumference and r is the radius.

Pi is:

  • irrational
  • ratio of circumference to diameter
  • awesome
